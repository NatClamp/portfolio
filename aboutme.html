Hey there,

I'm Natalie Clamp, a software-developer in training, currently based at Northcoders in Manchester, UK.

I spend most of my time with JavaScript, CSS3 and HTML5. In the coming weeks I'll be getting to grips with Node.js, Express, Mongo DB, as well as React and Redux. Hopefully, by spring 2019, I'll have built some pretty cool projects for you to see over on my Projects page.

After my undergraduate masters degree in Biology from the University of Sheffield, I ended up working in the science directorate at the glorious Kew Gardens in London, curating and digitising botanical content for Plants of the World online.

It was here that I realised my love for web design, and after attending a bunch of meet-ups and a CodeFirst: Girls course. Now comes the hard, but rewarding work, of learning a trade. But you'll still find me hugging any ginkgo trees (or ferns) you see.
